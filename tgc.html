THE GARDEN CITY

The Garden City is just 3km away from sathy main road located in Kurumbapalayam Kunnathur. In a total area of 14 acres, these reasonably priced villas offer all the amenities of more expensive options without breaking the bank.

Near Garden City are a number of highly regarded universities and colleges, many of which are specialised in engineering. A mere 9.7 kilometres separate the construction site from Coimbatore International Airport. Facilities of the kind of Kovilpalayam Medical Center (KMCH) are readily available.
